Thirty political parties fielding candidates in New York City this election

Permalink: 2013-11-02 17:58:00 by David Prager Branner in

We have elections in a few days in New York. The Voter Guide published by the New York City Campaign Finance Board lists thirty parties supporting at least one candidate in the election:

  1. Abiodun Lauren-Smith
    2. - this is the name of a Mayoral candidate
  2. Affordable Tmrw
    3. - associated with Mayoral candidate Joseph G. Melaragno
  3. Civic Virtue
  4. Common Sense
  5. Conservative
  6. Democratic
  7. Dump The Dump
    8. - associated with City Council candidate David Garland, as is the Save 2nd Avenue Party
  8. Faith and Values
  9. Flourish Every Person Can Shine Like the Sun
    10. - associated with Mayoral candidate Michael J. Dilger
  10. Forward Brooklyn
  11. Freedom
  12. Green Party
  13. Independence
  14. Jobs & Education
  15. Liberal
  16. Libertarian
  17. Populist
  18. Progressive
  19. Reform
  20. Rent is 2 Damn High
    21. - the long-running platform of [Jimmy (rent is too damn high) McMillan][]
  21. Republican
  22. Save 2nd Avenue
    23. - see Dump the Dump, above
  23. School Choice
  24. Socialist Worker
  25. Students First
  26. Tax Wall Street
    27. - associated with Mayoral candidate Randy Credico, who describes himself as a "New Deal Democrat"
  27. Taxes 2 High
    28. - John Catsimatidis's party
  28. Unity
  29. War Veterans
  30. Working Families

Twenty-one of these have a candidate running for one or another position in my City Council district.
