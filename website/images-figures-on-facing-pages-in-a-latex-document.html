Images (figures) on facing pages in a LaTeX document

Permalink: 2012-07-26 11:19:00 by David Prager Branner in

For figures on facing pages:

  1. Jim Fox's dpfloat package defines two environments, leftfullpage and fullpage, which force the figures onto a left page and the following right page. The documentation is simple and clear.
  2. Martin Scharrer produced a beautiful piece of code that divides a single image into two halves and spreads them across facing pages. See http://tex.stackexchange.com/a/23865/3935

Parallel text on facing pages can be handled with the parallel or parcolumns packages, and there are probably other solutions, too.
